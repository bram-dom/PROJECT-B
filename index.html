For one to qualify in the whole process of bursary application, the system will check and verify all details.

CDF bursaries are given to students with special needs and poor students who need to study but lack the fees. It was introduced by constituency act during the era of President Mwai Kibaki in 2003. alongside free educations goal of Mwai Kibaki, the bursary have abundantly assisted millions of students in Kenya complete their studies
